**HOW CAN WE TURN ANIMAL W ASTES INTO USEFUL PRODUCTS?**

What kind of animal wastes are high in commercial value?

Today, soil has been saturated with longtime continuous use of chemical fertilizer. Instead of chemical fertilizer, animal

wastes such as fowl, cow and pig droppings are now being preferred by most farmers because they are organic fertilizers.

Animal wastes are not good fertilizers when used in their raw form because of the unevenly distributed ingredients.

In some parts of the world, some farmers are using the animal waste as an organic fertilizer by diluting with water. The

liquified manure is then sprayed directly to the farmlands, although such forms of organic fertilizer maybe less harmful

than chemical, they pose threat to the environment. When the liquified animal manure is absorbed by the soil it also

contaminates the underground water.

But the fermentators developed by KOHSHIN ENGINEERING has the answer and the most advanced machines that can

completely ferment animal waste and produce organic fertilizer with high commercial value. Thus, our products help you

gain more profit in livestock and poultry farming. There are various types and sizes of fermentators that will suit your

needs. Ali of the machines can be operated manually and automatically.

**WHA T IS FERMENT A TION** ?

W ays of processing animal wastes :
To turn animal wastes into more effective compost with even distribution of fertilizer ingredients, it is essential that the
manure are fully fermented. It should be noted that, when an incomplete fermented compost is used as it is for cultivation,

it might harın the crops.

Efficient fermentation are dependent on three factors :
**1) Moisture**
For more efficient fermentation the moisture content must be adjusted between 65-70 %.
Example: By mixing with the dried product produced by KOHSHIN machines, rice husk, saw dust, waste from sugar

cane and ete.

**2) Oxygen Supply**

Fermentation is activated by chemical resolution caused by the increase in bacteria.To increase bacteria, a large quantity

of oxygen must be supplied. For this reason, accumulated animal wastes must be turned

over and mixed while being supplied with the air using a blower.

**3) Temperature**

To accelerate fermentation, temperature must be controlled properly. As oxygen is supplied and mixing is made, increase
in bacteria becomes more active and the temperature of the waste products goes up from 70-80 ° C in the course of

fermentation process.
