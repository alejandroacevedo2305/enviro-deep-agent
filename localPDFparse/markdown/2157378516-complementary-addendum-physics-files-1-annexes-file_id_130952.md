# _1 Product Description_

The DA 1700 wall fan is a flange-mounted corrosion-free fan with cone and motor-controlled shutter. The fan
is supplied in one version focused on low energy consumption and one version focused on maximizing air
output. Both versions are available in several variants.

DA 1700 is characterised by having a particularly tightly closed motorized shutter, which prevents unwanted
air movement when the fan is not in operation, and a direct-driven motor to reduce motor maintenance.

DA 1700 is designed especially for the demanding house environment both when it comes to climatic and
electrical impacts.
