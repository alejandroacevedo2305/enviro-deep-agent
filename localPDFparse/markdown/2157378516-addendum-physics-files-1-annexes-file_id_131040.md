**SCANIA IN GENERAL**

Scania constantly works with the environmental characteristics of its vehicles and lower
resource consumption and raise efficiency in its production system as much as possible.
Scania is implementing an Environmental Management System and is certified according to the
standard ISO 14001 (environment) and ISO 9001 (quality). Scania works with Environmental
Performance Indicators (EPI) e.g. for energy use, and chemicals. Scania’s environmental work
is described in the Scania Annual Report.

**SCANIA ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY**

Scania continuously improves the environmental performance of its products, processes and
services.
Business demands and other requirements form the basis for improvement, where fulfilment of
legislation is fundamental.

Scania's environmental work is proactive, based on a life-cycle perspective and the principle of
precaution.
