**2.1** **Test environment**

**Description of test environment:** Placed on the open ground, no reflectors within 10m

**Ambient temperature:** 10°C

**Ambient humidity:** 50%RH

ambient temperature.

|No.|Operating condition|
|---|---|
|1|MVS SCC operates at full power|

transformer does not operate.
