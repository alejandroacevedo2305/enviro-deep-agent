# 3 Technical Details of STS-6000K-H1

### 3.1 General Description of STS-6000K-H1

STS-6000K-H1 is a compact 20’ HC container that contains an outdoor transformer, MV switchgear
and LV panel. It enables a quick and reliable connection of PV inverter to the MV grids.

STS-6000K-H1 perfectly match Huawei SUN2000 1500V inverters, and STS-6000K-H1 has lower
load loss and no-load loss in accordance with EN50588-1

Seismic level of Huawei STS can be seismic level 8. All internal equipment are to be anchored to
floor/ walls. Seismic loads of Huawei STS can be 0.35g horizontal acceleration and 0.15g vertical
acceleration. Wind load of Huawei STS can be 52.4 m/s.

**3.4.4 Suggestions on Building Oil Tray**

Oil tray is used to receive the insulating oil of MV transformer in case of leakage, the volume
of the oil tray is recommended to be 120% of the transformer oil volume. Concerning STS6000K-H1, the weight of STS-6000K-H1 is no more than 22 t. and transformer oil is mineral
oil, noncorrosive. And the Volume of transformer oil is about 3850L (about 3.4t).

Oil tray is out of Huawei scope, and it is recommended to make concrete oil tray on site by
EPC, thus better matching concrete foundation, quicker delivery and cost saving.

**3.5.2 MV Switchgear Interlocking**

MV switchgear shall be equipped with standard MV switchgear interlocking as default.
Also, for STS equipped with DVC switchgear, STS shall be equipped with interlocking between
STS for higher personnel safety and availability as default.
The interlocking procedures are descripted as below:

1) Standard MV Switchgear Interlocking

The MV switchgear meets the standard Interlocking procedure: the disconnector switch can
be opened only when the circuit breaker is open; the disconnector switch can be closed
only when the earthing switch is open; the earthing switch can be closed only when the
disconnector switch is open; the door of cables cabinet can’t be opened until the earthing
switch closed; the earthing switch can be open only after the door of cables cabinet closed.

All earthing switch of MV switchgear are equipped with padlock to lock the earthing
switch.

2) Interlocking between STS

The interlocking between STS adopts the rigorous mechanical interlocking procedures. The
mechanical interlocking procedures are rigorous, thus preventing error operating onsite.
Also, via the mechanical interlocking, it is simple design and there is no need for extra
power supply to mechanical lock.

The interlocking procedures are descripted as below:

 - K1: mechanical lock for cable incoming unit of #1 STS. To obtain key K1 only when
earthing switch closed.

 - K2: mechanical lock for cable outgoing unit of #2 STS. To obtain key K2 only when the
unit door closed.

 - Keys of K1 and K2 shall be tied together with key ring that can’t be separated at will
onsite.

### 3.8 Accessories

**3.8.1 Internal Cabling**

All the internal cabling within the STS are prefabricated and pretested at the factory, including
busbar between LV panel and transformer, MV cable between transformer and MV
switchgear, communication cable and grounding cables. No further cabling work for internal

**3.8.2 Ventilation System**

Compared with traditional air-forced cooling solution via fans and filters, Huawei STS adopt
the optimal cooling design: no air-cross convection. The LV and MV room of STS are equipped
with heat exchanger for heat dissipation. (The LV room is equipped with 2 heat exchangers,
and MV room is equipped with 1 heat exchanger.)

The cooling operating principle is below:

From the operating principle of heat exchanger above, we can see that the internal
components are isolated from the external ambient environments by aluminum alloy layer.
Also, such design can guarantee the high availability of the internal component. Furthermore,
there is no air-cross between inside and outside air and no need for quick-wear parts - filters,
thus for easy maintenance.

Also, to prevent sky radiation, the ceilings of STS MV/LV room are designed with thermal
insulation layer.

Huawei STS has pass thermal simulation through thermal platform by Huawei dedicated
thermal engineering team.

**3.8.3 Anti-rodent system**

Huawei STS provides the cable inlet and fire-stop putty for incoming and outing cable sealing,
ensuring that MV room and LV room is IP54.

**3.8.4 Human Presence Detection System**

Huawei STS is equipped with limit switch. When the door open, the digital signal shall be
output and sent to Huawei SmartACU, and then to the remote management system.

**3.8.5 Lighting System**

Huawei STS is equipped with internal light system, and MV&LV room shall include a general
lighting system designed with ceiling lamps (IP00).

**3.8.6 Smoke Detection System**

Huawei STS is equipped with smoke detection system, and both MV and LV room are
equipped with smoke detection sensors. When the smoke detection sensor detects an
abnormality, it will send a signal to Huawei SmartACU.

### 3.9 Communications

As STS facilitating the monitoring and communication, Huawei STS support real time
monitoring of transformer, MV switchgear and LV panel, 0.2% high precision sensor of LV
electricity parameters and remote control of MV circuit breaker and LV ACB.

All the smart signals of STS (status and protection signals, high precision analog signals etc.)
aggregates in the measurement and control device in the STS LV panel, and then transmitted
via RS485 cable to Huawei SACU. Then all the signals can be transmitted to customer’s
SCADA or other the management system via the Modbus-RTU protocol by Huawei SACU.

For more detailed signals for STS, please refer to < Modbus Signals of Huawei STS>.
