### Fire Suppression System Control Logic

1. The smoke and heat sensors detect a fire signal, they will send the

alarm signal to the fire control panel.

2. The fire control panel activates the alarm bell to notify nearby

personnel to evacuate. If two or more sensors detect the fire signals,

the controller will create a secondary alarm signal, and starts the

audible and visual alarm immediately.

3. The jet delay timer will start countdown, and the timer is adjustable

from 0 to 30s by preference.

4. After the delay timer is started, the control system will shut down

the DC-DC main circuit, disconnect the contactor, and turn off the

air conditioner and fan.

5. After the delay expires, it starts the cylinder solenoid valve to spray

extinguishing agent and turns on the gas release indicator.
